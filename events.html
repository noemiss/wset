Next upcoming event:

Industry Networking Event - 26 October

We usually invite female speakers from different companies within SET industries to talk to Imperial College students about their careers, their journeys to date, and any specific experiences they may have encountered as women within male-dominated environments. We provide an informal, friendly setting in which students can chat to the speakers over food and drinks. Sponsors are invited to join us for this evening event and provide a speaker to give a talk if they wish to.

Future events:

TEDx Event

For next academic year, we are set on organising a public speaking to which to invite our sponsors and create a sharing environment. In 2015, we also held a TEDx Event which featured a speaker panel made up of prominent figures in different areas, who shared their ideas on how to tackle gender inequality in the workforce and their own career paths. With early planning we believe we can make the event impactful and wide-reaching to inspire the student body from Imperial College.
